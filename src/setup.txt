Setup
-----
In content/js/connection you also need to download the paramikojs source which is currently hosted in the fireftp source tree.

cd content/js/connection
cvs -d :pserver:guest@mozdev.org:/cvs login
cvs -z3 -d :pserver:guest@mozdev.org:/cvs co fireftp/src/content/js/connection/paramikojs
mv fireftp/src/content/js/connection/paramikojs/ .
rm -rf fireftp
